Welcome to Mall of Asia

The Mall of Asia (MOA) is one of the largest shopping malls in the Philippines, located in Pasay City. Opened in 2006, it has become an iconic destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment, attracting millions of visitors every year. With over 600 retail stores, numerous restaurants, and world-class entertainment venues, MOA has something for everyone.

Whether you are looking for the latest fashion trends, enjoying a family meal, or watching a blockbuster movie, Mall of Asia is the place to be. The mall also offers various leisure activities, including ice skating, an amusement park, and a sprawling outdoor space by the Manila Bay, perfect for a relaxing stroll while enjoying the beautiful sunset.

In addition, MOA hosts regular events, concerts, and festivals, making it a lively hub of activity throughout the year. It truly is a must-visit destination for both locals and tourists.
